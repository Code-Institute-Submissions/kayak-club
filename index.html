About Us

Who are we?

We are people who love our sport and enjoy white water and sea kayaking. Everyone came from the different background and have own path in life. We run the Club on voluntary basis for over 20 years.

What do we do?

During the summer months we run Beginner's Kayaking courses and continue to kayak throughout the winter months with official club trips to explore coast line or river adventures. On the regular bases we meet in our Clubhouse on river Lee.

Why paddle with us?

We take safety seriously. Everyone is look after and always recieve help. In our Clubhouse, we have a wide range of safety and kayaking equipment. We provide Rescue and Safety trainings for club members. Kayaking it's not only a great fun but a great exercise as well.
